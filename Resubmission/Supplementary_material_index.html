Title of paper: Transport map accelerated adaptive importance sampling, and application to inverse problems arising from multiscale stochastic reaction networks

Authors: Simon L. Cotter, Ioannis G. Kevrekidis, and Paul Russell

File: TETAIS_supplementary.pdf

Type: Text

Contents: Shows detailed derivation of the likelihood of inverse problems for stochastic reaction networks, including for multiscale networks with multiscale approximations.

Justification: The derivations were removed from the manuscript for brevity. The treatment is natural but isn't available in the literature to our knowledge. Hence we provide these details as supplementary material.
